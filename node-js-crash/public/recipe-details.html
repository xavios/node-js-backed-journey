Rainbow Smoothie - {{ RECIPE_UUID }}

A colorful, refreshing treat

Overview

This vibrant smoothie blends fresh fruits into a layered rainbow of flavor. Perfect for breakfast or a fun afternoon snack.

Prep Time: 10 min   |   Serves: 1–2

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • ½ cup strawberries
  • ½ cup mango chunks
  • ½ cup blueberries
  • ½ cup spinach
  • 1 cup yogurt or milk
  • Honey (optional)

Instructions

  1. Blend each fruit separately with a splash of yogurt or milk.
  2. Start with the heaviest mixture (usually banana or mango) and pour into a tall glass.
  3. Slowly layer each color on top using a spoon to soften the pour.
  4. Add honey if you want extra sweetness.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy the rainbow.

Fun Tip

Freeze the fruit beforehand for a thicker, slushier smoothie that holds its layers even better.
